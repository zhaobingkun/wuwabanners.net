How to handle official versus expected data

Use explicit labels. If the next phase is in an official notice, mark it as official. If it is inferred from drip marketing or release rhythm, mark it as expected. Mixing the two makes the page less trustworthy and harder to maintain.

  • Official: from official site notices, in-game Convene, patch notes, or official social posts.
  • Expected: based on banner history, version rhythm, or role marketing patterns.
  • Unconfirmed: keep leaks off the main line unless they are clearly separated.
