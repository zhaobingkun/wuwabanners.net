Wuthering Waves Weapons

A weapons page should not just be a giant list. It should help users understand weapon types, connect them to banner decisions, and send them to the right next page without making them dig through unrelated data first.

Direct answer: The best Wuthering Waves weapons page groups weapons by planning intent first, then routes users into weapon banner, items, and character pages depending on what they are trying to decide.

Banner-linked weapons

Users checking live or next weapons often arrive from banner pages, so the weapons page should clearly connect back to weapon banner context.

Upgrade-linked weapons

Upgrade planning intent should lead naturally into items and future weapon-material pages.

Character-linked weapons

Many users really mean “which weapons matter for the character I am pulling,” so character routes matter here.

FAQ

What should a Wuthering Waves weapons page focus on?

Weapon types, banner-linked weapon context, and the best routes into upgrade or character planning pages.

Why is a weapons page useful here?

Because weapon decisions often sit immediately behind a pull or save decision, especially around live and next weapon banner phases.
