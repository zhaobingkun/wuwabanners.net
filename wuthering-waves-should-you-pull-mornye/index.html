Should You Pull Mornye in Wuthering Waves?

Use this page to decide whether Mornye is worth pulling during the live Version 3.3 Phase 1 phase or whether saving is still the better call for your account.

Quick verdict: Pull Mornye if the live phase solves an immediate account need. Wait if your roster direction lines up more strongly with the next phase or a rerun target.

Last updated: May 19, 2026.

Phase reference video

Best for who

Best for accounts that want to invest in the current live phase instead of waiting for the next banner rotation.

Reasons to pull

Pull when Mornye is the clearest answer to your current roster gap and you are not preserving pity for the next phase lineup: Denia, Chisa, Phrolova.

Reasons to skip

Skip if you are already aiming at the next phase or holding resources for a rerun target that matters more.

Weapon and pity pressure

Do not rate Mornye only on character appeal. If the real cost question is whether the live weapon set Frostburn, Starfield Calibrator, Moongazer's Sigil stretches your pity too far, compare the full phase plan before spending.
