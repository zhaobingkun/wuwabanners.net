Should You Pull Denia in Wuthering Waves?

Use this page to decide whether Denia is worth saving for in the upcoming Version 3.3 Phase 2 phase instead of spending on the current live banner.

Quick verdict: Save for Denia if the next phase fits your roster plan better than the current banner. Spend now only if the live banner solves a more urgent account need.

Last updated: May 19, 2026.

Phase reference video

Best for who

Best for accounts planning around the next phase rather than using resources immediately in the current rotation.

Reasons to save

Save for Denia if your pity position and account plan already point toward the next phase instead of the current lineup: Hiyuki, Mornye, Iuno.

Reasons to wait longer

Wait even beyond Denia if your real target is a rerun unit and the next phase still does not match your account needs.

Weapon and pity pressure

Saving for Denia only wins if the next phase weapon set Forged Dwarf Star, Kumokiri, Lethean Elegy does not turn the plan into a worse pity trap than the live banner.

FAQ

Should you save for this next featured character?

Save when the upcoming phase fits your planned roster better than the current one and your pity state supports waiting.

What should you compare before deciding?

Compare the current banner end date, your pity progress, your rerun watchlist, and whether the upcoming phase solves a bigger problem for your account.

Can the next-phase weapon pressure change the answer?

Yes. A save decision is weaker if the next-phase weapon side makes the full plan less efficient than spending in the live phase or delaying for a rerun.
